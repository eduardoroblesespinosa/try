Features

Summon is a specialized workspace for developers that brings together your tools and notifications into one powerful experience.
Get your work done faster and be less stressed along the way.

Integrated workspaces
1/3

All-in-one workspaces to fit your workflow.

Instead of navigating an assortment of apps and browser tabs, Summon combines your calendar, code reviews, project management, builds and pipelines and more into flexible workspaces. Fit your tools to your workflow, not the other way around.

Unified activity feed
2/3

Know what's happening at a glance.

Get your notifications in one unified activities feed. No more jumping between email, Slack and web apps to figure out what needs your attention. See what’s happening at a glance and take action where needed.

Automated task list
3/3

Stay on top of your tasks. Automatically.

Always have an up-to-date list of priorities so you know what to work on next.
Whether it’s an upcoming meeting, reviewing a merge request or completing a Jira task, Summon helps you stay on top and prioritizes your daily work.
